üé§ Speech-to-Text System
An intelligent voice recognition system that converts spoken words into text, enabling hands-free typing and voice commands.
  • Microphone Integration: Continuously listens through the system microphone for voice input
  • Google Speech Recognition API: Utilizes Google's powerful speech recognition engine to accurately transcribe spoken phrases into text
  • Real-Time Processing: Processes audio in real-time with minimal latency for immediate feedback
  • Auto-Typing Feature: Optionally types recognized text directly into any active window or application
  • Multi-Language Support: Capable of recognizing multiple languages and accents
  • Error Handling: Gracefully handles background noise and unclear speech with retry mechanisms
